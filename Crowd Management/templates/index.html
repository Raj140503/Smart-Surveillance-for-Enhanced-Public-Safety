About the Project

Our system provides real-time crowd management by accurately counting the number of people entering and exiting a venue. Enhance safety, prevent overcrowding, and optimize space utilization with cutting-edge computer vision.

Efficient crowd management is essential for public safety and operational efficiency. Our solution is ideal for events, transit hubs, and public venues where monitoring flow is crucial.

Features

Process Video Files

Upload and process pre-recorded videos to analyze crowd flow. Our system detects and counts people crossing the entrance.

Real-Time Webcam Detection

Use your device's webcam for live crowd monitoring. Detect people in real-time with our advanced detection algorithms.

Implementation Details

Our system leverages advanced computer vision and deep learning techniques to ensure accurate people counting.

Computer Vision Techniques

We integrate state-of-the-art object detection models like YOLO and SSD with OpenCV to detect humans reliably in various conditions.

Virtual Counting Line

A virtual counting line is established at the entrance. As a person crosses this line, the system determines whether they are entering or exiting, ensuring an accurate tally of the total number of people currently inside.

Data Processing Pipeline

Our optimized pipeline processes each frame in real-time, running detection algorithms concurrently to maintain low latency for both pre-recorded videos and live webcam feeds.
